Hi, good to see you here!

My name is Aleksander Kattel and I am a student of Computer Science at the University of Tartu. I like to think of this website as my digital business card, where people can learn more about me.

I was born in Tartu, Estonia and have lived here my whole life. Which is 19 years to be exact. It's not a lot but I like to think of it as a good thing, because it means I still have a lot to explore.

As a person, my goal is to be as well-rounded as possible. I like to take on new things and I'm always looking for new opportunities to develop (pun intented) myself. I enjoy solving problems as well as being creative. My motto in life is 'Just keep playing'.

Oh, and one last thing, not to brag or anything, but I know the answer to the question 'How many holes does a straw have?'
